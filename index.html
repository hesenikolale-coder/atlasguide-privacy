1. Product Scope

AtlasGuide is a travel guide app that helps people evaluate countries, cities, neighborhoods, hotels, food spots, attractions, entertainment options, and trip ideas before travelling abroad.

This policy explains what data AtlasGuide may access, collect, use, share, retain, and delete.

2. Data AtlasGuide May Use

  • App settings such as language, onboarding choices, and travel preferences.
  • Saved places, active trip state, and offline trip cache created by the user.
  • Optional approximate or precise location, only when the user grants permission.
  • Product analytics events such as searches, itinerary views, saved places, and screen usage.
  • Crash diagnostics and technical error data when monitoring is enabled.
  • Affiliate or sponsored-content interaction events when a user opens a labeled partner link.
  • Advertising identifiers or ad SDK data only if advertising SDKs are enabled in a production release.

3. Why We Use Data

  • To personalize travel guidance and reduce irrelevant recommendations.
  • To keep saved places, trip plans, and offline content available.
  • To show nearby recommendations when the user allows location access.
  • To understand product quality, crashes, errors, and app performance.
  • To measure affiliate or sponsored recommendations transparently.
  • To support non-intrusive monetization if ads or affiliate links are used.

4. Location

Location is optional. If the user grants location permission, AtlasGuide uses location to show nearby recommendations and travel context. If location is denied or revoked, the app continues to work with curated editorial recommendations.

5. Analytics, Ads, And Partners

AtlasGuide uses product analytics only when allowed by the user consent settings in the app. If advertising, affiliate, or sponsored-content partners are added, those partners and their data practices must be reflected in this policy and in the App Store / Google Play privacy forms before release.

6. Sharing

AtlasGuide does not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers only when needed to operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, crash monitoring, maps/place data providers, affiliate partners, or ad networks if those integrations are enabled.

7. Security, Retention, And Deletion

AtlasGuide should use secure transport, restricted production keys, and limited access to production systems. User data should be retained only for the period needed to provide app functionality, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, or measure product quality.

Users can request support or deletion by contacting replace-with-real-support-email@example.com. Deletion requests should include enough information to identify the relevant app data, without sending sensitive documents unless requested through a secure support process.

8. User Choices

  • Users can change in-app consent preferences from the profile/privacy area.
  • Users can deny or revoke location permission from device settings.
  • Users can use the MVP without creating a personal account.
  • Users can request data deletion through the support contact above.

9. Children

AtlasGuide is not designed for children and should not knowingly collect personal data from children. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided data, they can contact the privacy email above.

10. Changes

This policy may be updated when AtlasGuide changes its data practices, partners, monetization model, or legal requirements. Store privacy forms and in-app disclosures should be kept consistent with the latest policy.
